Biography
Chunbin Lin is a database engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS) and he is working on AWS Redshift, which is a cloud data warehouse.

Before that, he worked as an intern at Amazon, Informatica, and IBM.

He received the PhD degree his Ph.D. in computer science at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), where he was co-supervised by Prof. Yannis Papakonstantinou. He obtained his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science Renmin University of China.
Research Interests
  • Database systems: distributed databases, row/column store, database compression, OLAP, OLTP
  • Data integration: entity matching, similarity join and search
  • Search engine systems: index, compression, top-k search, query optimization
  • Semi-structure query processing: XML index, XML keyword search, XML twig search
  • Approximate query processing: error-bounded OLAP, approximate IoT queries, lossy compression
