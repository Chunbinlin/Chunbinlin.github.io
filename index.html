Chunbin Lin

Biography
Chunbin Lin is a software engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS) and he is working on AWS Redshift, which is a petabyte-scale data warehouse service in the cloud. He completed his Ph.D. in computer science at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) under the supervision of Prof. Yannis Papakonstantinou in 2018. His research interests are Database Management and Information Retrieval. He obtained his B.S. and M.S. degrees from the School of Information at Renmin University of China (RUC) in 2010 and 2013, respectively.
